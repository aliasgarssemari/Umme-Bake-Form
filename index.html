Our Story

Crafted by Hand, Baked from the Heart

Umme Bakes started from a simple passion: to bring joy to people through the art of baking. What began in a home kitchen has blossomed into a beloved community bakery, but our philosophy remains the same.

We believe in quality over quantity. Every cake is a centerpiece, every pastry a small moment of happiness. We are dedicated to our craft and to you, our cherished customer.
